Getting Started with Your Telegram Bot Mini App

Follow these simple steps to get started quickly. Each step will be revealed as you scroll down.

Step 1: Start the Bot

Search for the bot on Telegram and click the "Start" button to initialize the bot.

Don't forget to click "Start" to interact with the bot!

Step 2: Open the Mini App

Click the menu icon on the bot and select "Mini App" to access the app.

Once you select it, the Mini App will open directly in Telegram.

Step 3: Enjoy the Features

Explore all the features of the Mini App and start using them to manage tasks, reminders, and more.

Experience the ease of using Telegram for your productivity needs.
